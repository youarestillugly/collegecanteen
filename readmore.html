About the Canteen

Welcome to the Sherubtse College Canteen

Our canteen is a vibrant place offering delicious and nutritious food options for students, staff, and visitors. We pride ourselves on serving a variety of dishes, catering to different tastes and dietary requirements.

Whether you're looking for a quick snack, a full meal, or refreshing beverages, our canteen has something for everyone. Enjoy your meals in a relaxed and friendly environment.

Our Services

What We Offer

  • Freshly prepared meals, snacks, and drinks.
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options available.
  • Discounted prices for students and faculty members.
  • Outdoor seating available for sunny days.
  • Free Wi-Fi for visitors to stay connected while dining.
